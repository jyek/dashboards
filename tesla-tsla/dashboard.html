Tesla (TSLA)

Electric vehicle and clean energy company. World's largest EV manufacturer by volume.

Business Model

  • Automotive (~85% revenue) — Vehicle sales (Model S/X, Model 3/Y, Cybertruck, Semi), regulatory credits, leasing
  • Energy Generation & Storage (~10%) — Megapack (utility-scale), Powerwall (residential), solar
  • Services & Other (~5%) — Supercharging, insurance, body shop, merchandise
  • Emerging — Robotaxi/FSD ride-hail fleet, Optimus humanoid robot (pre-revenue ramp)

Key Drivers

  • Vehicle deliveries — total units, model mix (S/X vs 3/Y vs Cybertruck), geographic split
  • Average selling price — ASP trends, pricing pressure vs mix shift to higher-end
  • Gross margin — auto gross margin excl. credits, energy margins improving rapidly
  • Energy storage — MWh deployed, ASP per kWh, growing as % of total revenue
  • New revenue streams — Robotaxi revenue, Optimus hardware + subscription revenue (2026E+)

Model Flow

Revenue & Deliveries (units by model, ASPs, energy deployed, robotaxi/optimus buildup) → TSLA (consolidated P&L: revenue, margins, EBITDA, EPS, SBC adjustments) → BS (balance sheet: cash, debt, equity, working capital) → CF (cash flows: OCF, capex, FCF, buybacks)

Currency

USD (millions). Calendar year reporting.
